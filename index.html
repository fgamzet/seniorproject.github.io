about us

Smart Spraying For Agricalture

In our senior project, we wanted to set up a system that we designed to reduce pesticide spraying in agriculture and to solve the farmers' infestation problem. As a result of our research, we have obtained a rough draft design. This design uses a chemical called pheromone to attract insects to a chamber. The image of the insects in the trap will be transferred to the farmer at regular intervals via the mobile application. In this way, the farmer can spray at a time he sees fit by giving command over the application.
